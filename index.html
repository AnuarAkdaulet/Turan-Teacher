About This Site

This site allows you to conduct tests, track student progress, and manage user profiles. You can navigate to different pages using the menu above.

Pages Overview

  • Dashboard (home.html): Centralized view for teachers to manage content.
  • Test (test.html): Take a test and view your results.
  • Login (login.html): Login with Google to access personalized features.
  • Privacy (privacy.html): View our privacy policy.
  • Callback (callback.html): Handles the Google login callback.
